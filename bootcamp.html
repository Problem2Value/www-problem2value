Innovation Bootcamp

Join our Innovation Bootcamp to learn about the whole innovation spectrum and become better at pitching your ideas.

What you will learn

During this 1-day workshop you will get an overview of most common innovation methodologies and how they complement eachother.

You will master how to structure and document your idea with the help of our Business Blueprint tool.

You will learn how to translate your idea into a convincing pitch with practical tips & tricks.

Day structure

- Overview of innovation methodologies, frameworks & techniques

- How to capture, document & prioritise an idea using the Business Blueprint.

- Complete your own Business Blueprint.

- Overview on how to translate a Business Blueprint into a convincing story.

- Pitch your idea.
