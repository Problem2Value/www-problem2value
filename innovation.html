Packages

We have seen many companies run innovation workshops which fail to have real impact.

We go much further. With our packages we  facilitate and guide you along the entire innovation lifecycle. From early problem space exploration, to designing & testing solution concepts, all the way to building and validating your Minimum Viable Product.

Research

The foundation of a successful innovation is a solid understanding of the problem. Our Research Package will help you discover who your customers are and what drives them.

Research can provide valuable insights for existing products as well as to explore new opportunity areas.

We will guide you beyond desk-research and focus on learning rather than trying to confirm pre-conceived assumptions.

Our Design Thinking inspired approach:
  • Define the research goal
  • Collect data
  • Translate the collected data into opportunity areas
  • Ideate and test potential solutions

Concept

You will design one specific aspect of your solution with a "fake it before you make it" prototype.

In a single week you will be able to find out if your solution is worth developing, or whether your value proposition is valid.

The concept package can validate in a fraction of the time (and cost) of what otherwise would take months to implement.

Our Design Sprint flavoured approach:
  • Set the focus of the design challenge
  • Sketch & share Ideas
  • Co-create and test a prototype
  • Validate or unvalidate assumptions

MVP

A specialist team will build your Minimum Viable Product to test with your early adopters.

Your product will be built by a self-organizing and cross-functional team using the Scrum agile framework. You will be able to attend regular sprint planning meetings to closely monitor progress.

Book our MVP package when you have achieved problem-solution fit and you are ready to validate solution-market fit.

Our Approach:
  • Build your dream team
  • Kickstart on-premise
  • Develop remotely using short sprints
  • Launch and test each sprint
