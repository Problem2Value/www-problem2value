Staffing

Get access to our pool of european-based remote UX Designers and IT Developers to develop your MVP.

Build your hybrid dream team!

Whether you need some extra help to build your MVP or you are looking to permanently increase your workforce, we have partners with the ideal solution for your needs.

Whilst we are advocates of remote work, we firmly believe regular, in-person contact is paramount for any team to be a real dream team. Our staff will attend an on-premise kickoff at the beginning of each new assignment. With longer assignments they will re-visit every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain the close bond with the rest of the team.

You are in control to select experts based on skilled level but also on cultural-fit. Our experts can work with your procedures, methods & tools, or if needed they can provide their own.
